Welcome to the Pokémon Trainer Club!

Gather with fellow Pokemon fans to do simple Pokemon crafts, watch an episode of Pokemons with us, or simply just play some Pokemon. Bring your own cards or use the Club set. Game play is self directed so it is preferable if children have some basic knowledge of the game. Suitable for Children ages 7 and up.


Pokémon (short for Pocket Monsters) are titular creatures that appear in the franchise of the same name. The franchise originated in Japan and has since become a worldwide phenomenon, branching out into all aspects of modern pop culture. Over the course of the franchise, there is a total of 905 species of Pokémon.

To learn more about Pokémon, visit Pokémon.

Pokémon universe


Pokémon are shown to exist instead of animals in their world. While most Pokémon resemble animals and may behave like them, there are many that do not resemble animals at all; taking on other forms such as plants, inanimate objects, machines, human-like forms or other more enigmatic and exotic appearances. Pokémon inhabit an extremely diverse range of habitats, ranging from the driest deserts to the lushest jungles, the deepest oceans to the highest mountains and everything else in-between, even space and other dimensions. Pokémon take up various ways of living in those places. However, all can be befriended by humans and made into potential allies.
