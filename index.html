Welcome to the Pokémon Trainer Club!

Gather with fellow Pokemon fans to do simple Pokemon crafts, watch an episode of Pokemons with us, or simply just play some Pokemon. Bring your own cards or use the Club set. Game play is self directed so it is preferable if children have some basic knowledge of the game. Suitable for Children ages 7 and up.


Pokémon (short for Pocket Monsters) are titular creatures that appear in the franchise of the same name. The franchise originated in Japan and has since become a worldwide phenomenon, branching out into all aspects of modern pop culture. Over the course of the franchise, there is a total of 905 species of Pokémon.

How to play Pokémon TCG?

Shuffle your deck. Your deck should have exactly 60 cards.
With coin determine who goes first. Take 7 cards from the top of the deck and put them aside. Draw a card from the top of your deck.
You may attach only 1 energy per turn to any of your Pokemon. You may evolve any Pokemon that have not been played that turn. You may use any abilities as many times as you wish unless stated otherwise. Once you are finished with your turn you may attack if you have the required energy count. You may not attack on your first turn, if you choose to go first.
